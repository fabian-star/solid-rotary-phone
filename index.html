The Rockton Mystery is an online treasure hunt game based in Rockton, Illinois! This challenge uses QR codes to direct you to webpages with images that point to more QR codes.

⚠The end of the challenge does get more difficult!⚠

Let's Go!

You probably arrived here by scanning a QR code somewhere. This puzzle will invole scanning more in the future, so a phone with QR code scanning capabilities is necessary. A computer is not necessary. You may need to rotate your phone to landscape phone at times to view images.

Beware of scanning random QR codes! ⚠ Some may be malicious and your phone could become compromised by scanning them. Always make sure you scan QR codes from trusted sources.
